Text Encoder/Decoder

How to use:

  1. Enter your text in the "Original Text" box
  2. Provide a secret key (remember this for decoding)
  3. Click "Encode" to encode your text
  4. To decode, paste the encoded text and enter the same key
  5. Click "Decode" to get your original text back

Note: You must use the same key for encoding and decoding.
